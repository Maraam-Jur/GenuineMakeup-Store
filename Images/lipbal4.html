      Full Description

      A treat to get or gift! Enjoy a soft-focus kiss of colour from lips to lids with a Pro-curated trio of Powder Kiss shades: Powder Kiss Lipstick in Devoted To Chili, Powder Kiss Liquid Lipcolour in Date-Maker and Powder Kiss Soft Matte Eye Shadow in Give A Glam. Online and in-store for $43 ($65 value). This item is not eligible for discounts or promotions.

      Shade Description
      • Lipstick: Devoted to Chili
      • Liquid Lipcolour: Date-Maker
      • Soft Matte Eyeshadow: Give A Glam
      Key Claims and Benefits
      • POWDER KISS LIPSTICK
      • Immediate and long-term hydration
      • Blurs lines and smooths lips
      • Lightweight
      • POWDER KISS LIQUID LIPCOLOUR
      • Immediate and 10-hour moisture
      • Long-wearing, 10 hours
      • Blurs lines and smooths lips
      • Non-settling, non-creasing, non-flaking, non-drying
      • POWDER KISS SOFT MATTE EYE SHADOW
      • Stay-true colour
      • Non-fading, non-creasing, non-drying
      • Water-resistant

      Recommended Usage
      • POWDER KISS LIPSTICK
      • Apply Lipstick directly to lips.
      • Reapply throughout the day as needed.
      • POWDER KISS LIQUID LIPCOLOUR
      • Apply directly to lips as desired with the hourglass-shaped applicator that perfectly hugs lips to fill in lines and enhance lips with a smoothing and blurring effect.
      • POWDER KISS SOFT MATTE EYE SHADOW
      • Apply to lids using a 286S Brush.
